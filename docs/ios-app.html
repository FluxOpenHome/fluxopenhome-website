iOS App

Flux Controller for iOS

Control your irrigation from anywhere. Works with both Home Assistant and Direct Connect controllers.

Download on the App Store

Requires iOS 16.0 or later. iPhone and iPad.

Full Control in Your Pocket

Everything you need to manage your irrigation system from your iPhone.

Zone Control

Start, stop, and schedule individual zones. See real-time status for every valve in your system.

Smart Scheduling

Set up to 4 start times per day. Configure run durations, repeat cycles, and rain delays.

Weather Monitoring

Live weather data integration. See forecasts and let the system skip watering on rainy days.

Gophr Readings

Monitor soil moisture at three depths. View probe status, battery level, and historical data.

Push Notifications

Get alerted for system events: watering complete, rain sensor triggered, probe readings, and issues.

Multi-Property

Manage multiple irrigation systems from one app. Switch between properties with a tap.

Works With Both Platforms

One app for both Home Assistant and Direct Connect controllers.

Home Assistant

Connect via Nabu Casa remote access or local network. Full integration with the Flux Irrigation Add-on. All features available locally.

Direct Connect

Connect directly to the Flux cloud management server. No Home Assistant required. Perfect for standalone and commercial installs.
