Controller Remote Setup Guide

1

Choose Your Variant

Two hardware options are available: the M5Stack Dial (240x240 round display with rotary encoder) and the Elecrow CrowPanel 2.1" (480x480 larger display with capacitive touchscreen). Both are $99.99.

2

Mount & Power On

Attach the wall-mount bracket in a convenient location near your irrigation controller. Connect power via USB-C. The display initializes with the Flux interface.

3

Connect to WiFi

On first boot, the remote advertises via Bluetooth. Open HA Companion app → Settings → Devices & Services and configure WiFi. Fallback: captive portal hotspot with no password.

4

Add to Home Assistant

The remote is auto-discovered by Home Assistant. Configure the device. All control entities sync bidirectionally with your Irrigation Controller.

5

Pair with Your Controller

The remote automatically syncs with your Flux Irrigation Controller through Home Assistant. Zone controls, durations, and schedules are mirrored in both directions — changes on the remote appear in HA and vice versa.

6

Navigate the Interface

The remote has several pages: Status (system overview), Zones (start/stop individual zones), Duration (set zone run times), Schedule (configure 7-day weekly schedule with 4 start times), and Settings (12/24-hour time format). Use the rotary encoder on M5Stack or swipe on CrowPanel to navigate.
