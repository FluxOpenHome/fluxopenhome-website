Built in the Open

Every Flux product ships with full source code. No locked-down firmware, no mandatory cloud subscriptions, no vendor lock-in. Your hardware runs on your network, under your control.

We provide the circuit boards, the firmware, and the 3D print files. You decide how to build it, how to run it, and how to extend it.
