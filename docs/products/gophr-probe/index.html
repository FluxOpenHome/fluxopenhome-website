Features

3-Depth Sensing

Measure soil moisture at shallow, mid, and deep levels for a complete picture of your soil profile.

Solar Charging

Built-in solar panel keeps the battery topped up for long-term outdoor deployment.

Deep Sleep

Wakes periodically to take readings, then returns to deep sleep to conserve battery.

HA Add-on Integration

Feeds moisture data to the Flux Home Assistant Add-on for moisture-based watering decisions.

WiFi or Cellular

WiFi version for areas with coverage. Cellular version for remote locations far from the controller — no WiFi needed.

Battery Monitoring

Real-time battery level and charging status reported to Home Assistant.

Smart Watering Integration

How Gophr works with the Home Assistant Add-on for automated watering decisions.

1

Gophr Reads Soil

The probe wakes from deep sleep and measures moisture at three depths, then transmits the data over your chosen protocol.

2

Add-on Processes Data

The Flux Home Assistant Add-on receives moisture readings and compares them against your configured thresholds.

3

Controller Waters

If moisture is below threshold, the Add-on triggers the Flux Irrigation Controller to water the appropriate zones.
